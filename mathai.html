Matemáticas de la IA

Dreams have fascinated humanity for centuries. Science now shows they play crucial roles in memory consolidation, emotional processing, and creative problem-solving.

Key Concepts

  • Neural Network: A system inspired by the human brain that processes information through interconnected layers to recognize patterns.
  • Graph Theory: A branch of mathematics that maps relationships using nodes and edges, often used in recommendation systems.
  • Game Theory: A mathematical framework for analyzing strategic decisions and understanding complex interactions.
  • Multivariable Calculus: A field of mathematics used to optimize and update millions of AI parameters simultaneously.
