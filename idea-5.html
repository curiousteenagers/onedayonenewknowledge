The Story of Pangaea

Pangaea was a massive supercontinent that existed hundreds of millions of years ago. Its formation and breakup reshaped Earth's geography, climate, and the evolution of life on our planet.

Key Concepts

  • Cratons are the ancient and stable foundations of Earth's continents.
  • The Permian-Triassic Extinction eliminated about 90% of species on Earth.
  • The Central Atlantic Magmatic Province played a major role in Pangaea's breakup.
  • Divergent evolution occurred as species became isolated on separate continents.
