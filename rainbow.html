Why Eyes Get Puffy?

Discover how fluid retention, sleep position, and the body's natural processes can cause swollen eyes in the morning.

Key Concepts

  • Periorbital Edema: The medical term for fluid retention in the soft tissues surrounding the eye area.
  • Orbital Cellulitis: A dangerous and deep infection that requires urgent medical treatment with antibiotics.
  • Skin Elasticity: The skin's natural ability to stretch and bounce back, which diminishes as people age.
  • Lymphatic System: The body's internal drainage network used to move trapped fluids away from the face.
